## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are unmanaged switches?  
Unmanaged switches are plug‑and‑play Ethernet switches with fixed ports that require no configuration, ideal for straightforward network deployments.

### Where are unmanaged switches used?  
They are commonly used in small businesses, offices, industrial sites, and surveillance networks requiring cost‑effective and reliable connectivity.

### Do unmanaged switches support PoE?  
Yes — select Versitron unmanaged models offer PoE/PoE+ to power devices like IP cameras and access points.
